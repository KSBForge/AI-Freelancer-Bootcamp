# Day 3 - Hallucinations

## What is a hallucination?

A hallucination is when an AI gives an answer that sounds confident but is false, made up, or unsupported.(The AI isn't trying to lie. It is predicting the most likely response based on patterns, and sometimes those predictions are wrong.)


## Why do hallucinations happen?

When AI have lack of realible information and it start guessing.
#mistake- Hallucinations happen when the AI does not have enough reliable information or certainty and predicts a likely answer instead of saying it doesn't know.

## Why are hallucinations dangerous?

For me as a AI freelancer hallucinations might damage my clients order and break his/her trust.

## How can we reduce hallucinations?

By verifying the given information, tell AI if you dont know enough information dont guess and answer confidentely, If you are uncertain about any fact, clearly say that you are not sure rather than making assumptions.
