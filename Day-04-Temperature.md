# Day 4 - Temperature

## What is Temperature?

Temperature is a setting that controls how creative or predictable an AI's answer will be.

## Low Temperature

Low Temperature means the AI gives more accurate, focused, and predictable answers.

When should we use it?

When we want Step-by-step instructions, accurate, focused and predictable answer.

Give 3 examples.
Python code
Math
Reports

## High Temperature

High Temperature means the AI gives more creative, unique, and varied answers.

When should we use it

When we want many different ideas.

Give 3 examples.
Stories
Business ideas
Marketing slogans
