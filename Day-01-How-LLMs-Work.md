# Day 1 - How LLMs Work

## What is an LLM?

LLM is a Large Language Modle which doesn't ask your questions on internet insted it predicts next words or (tokens) of your prompt.

## What is a Token?

A token is a small unit of text. (example: I respect you)

## Why do prompts matter?

Prompts matter because it gives AI enough information what we want to do with AI. A good prompts gives exactly details or information which AI analyse and in response it gives exactly what we need
